The "S.P.E.C." Framework

Before you buy your next prompt, try this formula. A high-performing prompt must have:

  • S - Situation: Assign the AI a persona (e.g., "You are an expert copywriter").
  • P - Problem: State the specific hurdle (e.g., "My ads feel boring").
  • E - Expectation: Define the exact output (e.g., "Write 5 hooks").
  • C - Constraint: Tell it what not to do (e.g., "Don't use corporate jargon").
